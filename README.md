## 📋 About This Portfolio

This is my professional portfolio website, built to showcase my journey as a **Business Analyst** with a **Computer Science Master's** background and **3 years of research experience at imec**. The site highlights my BA skills, technical expertise, certifications, and case studies.

**Live Site:** [https://vidya-portfolio-alpha.vercel.app/](https://vidya-portfolio-alpha.vercel.app/)

---

## 🚀 Features

- **Two-Column Layout** – Sticky navigation sidebar with scrollable content
- **Active Link Highlighting** – Left dash indicator shows which section you're viewing
- **Hover Highlight Effect** – Hovering over a section dims others and highlights the active one
- **Professional Styling** – Clean, recruiter-friendly design with custom teal accent color
- **Fully Responsive** – Works on desktop, tablet, and mobile devices
